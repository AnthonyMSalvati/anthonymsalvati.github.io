Rebuild Together is the perfect place for people to come after
disaster strikes. Our mission is to make sure that everyone is
able to get back up on their feet, have a place to stay, and
try to resume a normal life. Our hope is that through this
website, we can reduce housing inequality by giving people
some much needed help after a cataclysmic event.




Why our mission matters:


According to the US Census, after each natural disaster, hundreds of thousands
of people are displaced from their homes and end up moving into apartments.
The problem is, people arent moving back into homes because it's becomming too expensive.
Mortgage rates are increasing just as fast as rent rates, and the high price of rent
means people cannot afford to save up for a home. This widens the gap between
those with wealth and those without, as those with can purchase a house and have
the assets. Those renting apartments long term get no return from doing so, putting
them at a higher and higher defecit the longer they don't own a home.
