Why donate to us?
The housing crisis is not going to solve itself. Money from donations can go to lobbying
for better legislation to stimulate the housing market and regulate the unbridled growth
of rent prices. Proceeds from donations will also go to helping those in need. Any little
bit could be the difference between a family of four squatting under a bridge or having a
temporary shelter to keep them warm. Donations are used to provide housing for people who
are victims of the ever-skyrocketing prices of one of the most basic necessities.
